I love taking photos on film.

There’s something about the tactile nature that really attracts me. I like being able to look at a negative and hold the image in my hand. It gives me solace that if the world’s internet and electricity were to fail, I would still be able to look at my photographs. I’m heavily inspired by nature and the way that the natural world mixes with the man made.
